No.1 Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • Pav bhaji masala
  • Potatoes
  • Peas
  • Bread
  • Tomatoes

Procedure

For the mashed vegetables: Take a vessel and pour all the boiled vegetables inside it. Mash completely and keep aside.

For the bhaji: Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and fry until golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cook 3–4 minutes. Add 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste and cook until masala releases oil. Add spices (turmeric, chilli powder, salt, garam masala, pav bhaji masala) and mix. Add the mashed vegetables and a little water to achieve a medium-thick gravy.

For the pav: Heat a tawa, grease with butter and toast the pav 40–50 seconds per side.

Plating: Serve pav and bhaji together, garnish with lemon slices, chopped onion and coriander, and a knob of melted butter.

No.2 Pizza

Ingredients

  • Oregano
  • Chilli flakes
  • Pizza base
  • Veggies

Procedure

Spoon on tomato sauce, sprinkle with cheese, add desired toppings. Sprinkle cornmeal on the baking stone. Slide pizza onto the stone and bake until crust is browned and cheese is golden (about 10–15 minutes). Add extra cheese toward the end if desired.
